This project involves a comprehensive analysis of customer demographics, transactions, and purchasing behavior using SQL. The objective is to gain insights into customer segmentation, purchase trends, product performance, and potential cross-sell opportunities. The analysis includes profiling customers based on factors like gender, state, job industry, wealth segment, and ownership of assets like cars. Additionally, the project examines sales trends, order status, and brand performance, alongside identifying high-value customers and top-selling products. The results provide actionable data to enhance marketing strategies, customer engagement, and overall business performance.
